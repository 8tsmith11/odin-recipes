Chicken Marsala

Description

A classic Italian-American dish featuring tender chicken breasts sautéed and simmered in a rich, savory sauce made with Marsala wine, mushrooms, and herbs.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken
  • 1/2 cup marsala wine
  • 12 oz chicken broth
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cups breadcrumbs
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Filet chicken and pound for tenderness.
  2. Dip chicken in egg and drag both sides through breadcrumbs
  3. Fry in pan with olive oil until cooked.
  4. Place in a baking pan, add wine and broth.
  5. Cover and bake at 350°F for about 15 mins.
