Chicken Marsala

Description

Beef, rice, and scrambled eggs with spicy seasoning

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 2 cups brown rice
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 heaping tbsps cumin
  • 2 heaping tbsps chili powder
  • 1 tbsp cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Cook brown rice according to package directions.
  2. In seperate skillet, brown the ground beef.
  3. Drain the ground beef.
  4. Scramble eggs skilelt.
  5. Return beef to the skillet with the cooked eggs.
  6. Add spices, mix thoroughly.
  7. Add and mix rice when serving.
