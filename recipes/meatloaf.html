Meatloaf

Description

A hearty comfort food made with seasoned ground meat, shaped into a loaf, and baked to perfection, often topped with a tangy tomato glaze.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs ground beef
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1 packet dry onion soup mix
  • 3 tbsps ketchup

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until thoroughly blended.
  2. Form into a loaf shape.
  3. Bake at 350°F for 1 hour or until internal temperature is 160°F.
